# history — Historical knowledge and academic history tasks

## Purpose
Rules for answering historical questions, creating history-related educational content,
and supporting ЕГЭ/ОГЭ exam preparation.

Covers: historical facts/dates/figures, Russian and world history, academic history content,
exam prep materials (ЕГЭ/ОГЭ history).
Does NOT cover: science/tech history (→ ./science.md), current events/news.

## Rules & Directives

### Accuracy requirements
- History facts are 🔴 high-importance — confidence threshold 0.1
- For specific dates, names, and event sequences: always verify against authoritative sources
  before stating as fact
- Distinguish clearly between established historical fact and contested/interpretive claims
- For Russian history specifically: cross-reference Russian-language sources when available,
  as Western sources may have different framing on the same events

### ЕГЭ/ОГЭ exam prep context
- Tasks for exam prep should align with the official ФИПИ (Federal Institute of Pedagogical Measurements) syllabus
- Use standard Russian history periodization and terminology as used in school curriculum
- When generating test questions: mirror the format and difficulty level of actual ЕГЭ/ОГЭ tasks
- Matching tasks must include distractors (see content/cloudtext for publishing rules)

### Content generation for Dasha (163037943)
- Dasha uses this domain for creating educational materials for her classes
- Source materials come from her lecture notes; expand and structure them for task generation
- Follow content/cloudtext workflow: draft → approval → publish
- Combine related historical topics into thematic sections when preparing full test sets

### Source reliability
- Primary: official textbooks used in Russian school curriculum
- Academic: university-level Russian history sources
- Cross-check dates and figures; do not rely on Wikipedia alone for precise facts
