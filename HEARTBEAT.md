## Cadence
- Quiet hours: 23:00–08:00 local time.
- No more than 1 proactive ping per ~3 hours unless urgent.
- Nothing new → reply HEARTBEAT_OK.

## What to follow up on
Only if discussed recently and still relevant:
1. Planned actions with timing.
2. In-progress tasks needing a status check.
3. Risky/fragile setups.
4. Promised returns ("we'll come back to this").

## Message style
Short, human, optional-invitation. No templates.
